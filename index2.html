Veggie Pizza

If you are looking for low calorie food options to help you lose or maintain weight, then a veggie pizza is a much better idea. Meats are higher in fat and protein than veggies, which makes them more calorie dense than vegetables

Cheese Pizza

Cheese is fatty, meat toppings tend to be rich, and the sauce is sweet. Pizza toppings are also packed with a compound called glutamate, which can be found in the tomatoes, cheese, pepperoni and sausage.

Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni slices add flavor to a slice of pizza, but they aren't a nutritious topping. The cured meat is high in saturated fat and sodium, and it contains protein, but it doesn't supply large doses of essential nutrients
